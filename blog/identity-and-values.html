Identity, Values and Strengths

The Hanged Man (XII) is the twelth major arcana and is meant to represent self-sacrifice, a period of acceptance and reflection.

During this exercise of self evaluation it is this result from the many personality and aptitude tests that struck a chord with me.

"It is what it is"

"It can't be helped"

"Que sera, sera"

For as long as I can remember these have been my mantra.

But now I find myself with an opportunity to break free from this comfortable but unfulfilling life.

So what will it take to be successful in this new endeavor? How can I utilize my strengths? How can I mitigate my weaknesses?

Strengths

  • Optimistic - Not easily stressed. Able to undertake tasks without being overwhelmed and can reassure others.
  • Adaptive and Rational - Can switch mindsets to fit new situations easily. Stay versatile and flexible.
  • Analytic - Identify the root cause of the issue and work through it step by step.

Weaknesses

  • Reserved - An introvert through and through. But many hands make light work, remember that.
  • Uncommittal - Can't let them down if you don't promise anything right? Make a commitment so you're held accountable, or you'll only let down yourself.
  • Easily Bored - Stay focused, don't get distracted. Bring the fun into your learning, create what you want to create.

As I continue down this new journey I need to be aware of these points.
